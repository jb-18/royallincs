Our Founder

Meet our founder, Schlaria Brown. A hardworking mother of two who went out on her own to start her own business. While it's a long story for why she went into this field, to put it simply, she wanted to help out the best way she can.

Our Mission

Hello, my name is Schlaria Brown. I am the owner of Royallincs LLC. Which is a dispatching/freight transportation company. While researching the freight business, I found out that only 9.5% of truck drivers are women. And less than 5% of dispatchers are black women. My goal is to help expand not only women, but black women in the trucking business.

Dispatching Capabilities

We offer reliable and efficient dispatching to keep your trucks moving. From load booking and route planning to driver support and updates, we handle the logistics so you can stay focused on the road. Our team works closely with carriers to ensure smooth communication and on-time deliveries.
